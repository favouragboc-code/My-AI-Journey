💡
Key Anomaly: Calabar (58 AQI) outranks Port Harcourt despite less industry — seasonal biomass burning and cooking fuel elevate local particulates.

Seasonal Note: Current readings reflect rainy season (Jun 2026) — AQI is 30–50% lower than dry-season peaks (Nov–Feb) when harmattan dust dominates.
